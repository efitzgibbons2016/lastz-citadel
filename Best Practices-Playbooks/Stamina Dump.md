# Stamina Dump

**Playbook:** How to run coordinated stamina dumps in LastZ to generate hundreds of alliance gifts in a short window and raise Gift Level fast.

---

## What a Stamina Dump Is

In **LastZ**, **stamina** is the energy used to attack zombies, Doom Elites, or start rallies. A **stamina dump** is when **many alliance members spend all (or most) of their saved stamina at the same time**.

**Purpose**

- Generate **a large number of alliance gifts quickly**
- Increase **Alliance Gift Level**
- Farm **loot and rewards faster**
- Keep **rallies running continuously**

Instead of everyone playing at random during the day, the alliance **synchronizes attacks into a short window (often 30–60 minutes)**.

---

## How the “Stamina Dump Rally Chain” Works

*Top-alliance method in 5 steps.*

### Step 1 — Save Stamina

Members **do not spend stamina all day**. They save it for the scheduled dump.

**Common sources**

- Natural stamina regeneration  
- Daily rewards  
- Stamina items  
- Event rewards  

Many alliances ask members to **save 200–500 stamina** before the dump.

---

### Step 2 — Schedule a Rally Window

**Example**

| Setting   | Example        |
|----------|-----------------|
| **Time** | Server 20:00    |
| **Duration** | 45 minutes  |
| **Target**   | Doom Elites / Rally Bosses |

Everyone logs in at that time.

---

### Step 3 — Multiple Rally Leaders Start Chains

Strong members start **continuous rallies**. Members **join every rally as soon as it’s up**.

**Example flow**

1. Leader 1 starts rally → members join  
2. Leader 2 starts rally → members join  
3. Leader 3 starts rally → members join  
4. Leader 4 starts rally → members join  

When one rally finishes, **another starts immediately**. That creates a **non-stop rally chain**.

---

### Step 4 — Burn Stamina Quickly

Members use stamina on:

- Doom Elites  
- Zombie bosses  
- Rally targets  
- Event monsters  

Because **many people attack at the same time**, the alliance generates **many gifts in a short window**.

---

### Step 5 — Claim Gifts During the Dump

Tell everyone to **claim gifts during the dump**, not only after.

- Gift level progress increases when gifts are **claimed**.
- Everyone gets resources and speedups right away.

---

## Why This Works So Well

| Play style     | Scenario                          | Typical result   |
|----------------|------------------------------------|------------------|
| **Normal play** | 40 members attack at random all day | ~30 gifts        |
| **Stamina dump** | 40 members attack in 45 minutes     | ~120+ gifts      |

The **rapid burst** raises alliance gift progress much faster than scattered play.

---

## Ideal Alliance Setup for Dumps

Best results when you have:

- **3–6 strong rally leaders** (can rotate so there’s no downtime)  
- **30+ active members online** for the window  
- Everyone with **300+ stamina saved** (or 200–500 per your rule)  
- **Fast rally joiners** — join within seconds of rally start  

---

## Pro Alliance Tricks

**1. Rally rotation**  
Leaders take turns starting rallies so there is **zero downtime** between rallies.

**2. Discord / chat countdown**  
Example:

```
Dump in 5 minutes — prep stamina
Join rallies as soon as they go up
```

**3. Doom Elite focus**  
Doom Elites give **better rewards and gift chances** than normal zombies. Prioritize them when possible.

---

## Signs of a Strong Alliance

Alliances that climb gift levels quickly usually have:

- **Daily stamina dumps** at a set time  
- **Scheduled rally hours** so everyone knows when to be on  
- **Clear inactivity rules** (e.g. kick if missing dumps)  
- **Fast rally joins** (e.g. under 10 seconds)
