# Raise Alliance Gifting Level

**Playbook:** How to increase your alliance’s Gift Level in LastZ through activity, coordination, and smart claiming.

---

## What Is Alliance Gift Level?

In **LastZ**, your **Alliance Gift Level** increases when your alliance **generates and claims Alliance Gifts**. The level reflects how active (and, in some cases, how much spending) your alliance has. Raising it comes down to doing more of the actions that produce those gifts.

*Source: [First Fun Group – Alliance Gift](https://firstfungroup.zendesk.com/hc/en-us/articles/39668648978195-What-is-the-Alliance-Gift-How-does-it-work)*

---

## Fastest Ways to Raise Alliance Gift Level

### 1. Kill Doom Elites / Rally Bosses *(best for F2P)*

- Every time members defeat **Doom Elites or similar event enemies**, the alliance earns **regular gifts**.
- More kills → more gifts → faster gift level progress.

**Best strategy**

- Run **constant alliance rallies**.
- Coordinate **stamina dumps** at set times (e.g. after reset).
- Use **multiple rally leaders** so rallies don’t stop.

---

### 2. Members Buying Packs *(fastest overall)*

- When any member buys packs that include **Alliance Gifts**, the whole alliance gets **Premium Gifts**.
- Premium Gifts give much more gift progress than normal gifts.

**Reality on most servers**

- Alliances with **big spenders** climb gift levels much faster.

---

### 3. Claim Every Gift

- Gift level only goes up when **members actually claim the gifts**.
- Unclaimed gifts sitting in the inbox slow progress.

**Best practice**

- Remind members to **collect gifts multiple times per day**.

*Source: [Last Wars](https://www.lastwars.net/home)*

---

### 4. Run High-Activity Events

Take part heavily in:

- Alliance events  
- Zombie hunts  
- Rally events  
- Seasonal events  

More participation = more gift drops.

*Source: [Playbite – Alliance Gifts in Last Z](https://www.playbite.com/q/how-do-i-get-more-alliance-gifts-in-last-z-survival-shooter)*

---

## Alliance Leader Strategy (What Top Alliances Do)

To push gift level **very fast**, top alliances typically:

1. **Schedule daily rally hours** so everyone burns stamina together.
2. Set a minimum: members must **kill X Doom Elites per day**.
3. **Remove inactive players** to keep the roster active.
4. **Recruit active or spending players** to boost gifts and claims.

---

## Simple Formula

| More…           | = Higher gift level |
|-----------------|---------------------|
| Rallies         | ✓                   |
| Kills           | ✓                   |
| Spending (packs)| ✓                   |
| Gift claiming   | ✓                   |

**More rallies + more kills + more spending + more gift claiming = higher gift level.**
