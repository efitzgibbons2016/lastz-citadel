# Communications

A **reference folder** for alliance messages and announcements—your go-to spot for copy you can grab, tweak, and send.

Use it to keep a growing collection of messages for notifying your alliance about:

- Alliance info and updates  
- Events, schedules, and reminders  
- Whatever else you find yourself sending often  

Nothing formal about it: drop in templates, paste your best announcements, and reuse whatever works. Think of it as a shared clipboard that gets better the more you add.

**Grab, adapt, send.** That’s it.
