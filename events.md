# LastZ Game Events

*Event reference by game lifecycle, with event type, prerequisites, requirements, and strategies. Use with the alliance [Strategy](strategy.md) and [Org Structure](org-structure.md).*

---

## Event Type Key

| Type | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Repeating (Daily)** | Occurs every day; part of the core daily loop. |
| **Repeating (Weekly)** | Occurs on a fixed weekly schedule or cycle. |
| **Repeating (Biweekly)** | Occurs every 2 weeks; tied to SVS/State Ruler cycle. |
| **Repeating (Multi-day cycle)** | Runs on a multi-day rotation (e.g., 6-day Alliance Duel theme). |
| **Seasonal / Limited** | Appears for a limited calendar period; may recur in future seasons. |
| **One-off / Special** | Single occurrence or rare special event (e.g., launch, holiday). |

---

## Game Lifecycle Overview

Events are ordered by how they appear in a player’s and alliance’s journey:

1. **Early game** — Tutorial, first shelter upgrades, first research. No event prerequisites yet.
2. **Daily loop** — Full Preparedness (4‑hour themes), merit chests, FuryLord, daily tasks. Repeating daily.
3. **Weekly cycle** — Alliance Duel (6‑day theme rotation), Zombie Siege, Tyrant, Canyon Clash, Arena, hot events (e.g., Gacha Go, Lucky Discounter). Repeating weekly or on fixed days.
4. **Biweekly / SVS** — State Ruler prep phase (Sun–Fri), Capital Clash (Sat). Repeating every ~2 weeks.
5. **Seasonal / One-off** — Survival Saga, holiday or special events. Limited-time or one-time.

## 1. Daily Loop Events (Repeating Daily)

### 1.1 Full Preparedness

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Daily) |
| **Schedule** | Every day; 5 themes rotate every **4 hours**: Shelter Upgrade, Age of Science, Mod Vehicle Boost, Hero Initiative, Army Expansion. |
| **Prerequisites** | Shelter level (matchmaking is by similar level); resources/items for the active theme. |
| **Requirements** | Complete tasks in the current 4‑hour theme; earn points for personal milestones and ranking. |
| **Themes (task examples)** | **Shelter:** structure power, construction speed-ups. **Age of Science:** tech power, research speed-ups. **Mod Vehicle Boost:** blueprints, wrenches, boomers/creeps. **Hero Initiative:** Prime Recruit, EXP. **Army Expansion:** train/assemble/promote units, training speed-ups. |

**Strategies for winning / optimizing**

- Save resources and items for the theme that matches the current 4‑hour window (and ideally the Alliance Duel day).
- Use **orange / highest-point actions first** (e.g., Golden Wrench 600 pts in Mod Vehicle Boost).
- **Mod Vehicle Boost** and **Hero Initiative** are often easier to complete daily with minimal spend.
- Never spend key items (wrenches, speed-ups, fragments) outside event windows—align with Alliance Duel day and Full Preparedness theme.
- During SVS prep, Full Preparedness contributes 500+ state points; prioritize completion.

### 1.2 Merit Chests (Daily Tasks)

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Daily) |
| **Schedule** | Daily reset; complete event tasks to unlock chests. |
| **Prerequisites** | None. |
| **Requirements** | Complete enough tasks to open all 3 merit chests (typically 3+ tasks). |

**Strategies**

- Complete **all 3 chests** every day for maximum value.
- Stack with Full Preparedness: complete at least 3 tasks (e.g., Hero Initiative, Mod Vehicle Boost) for “free” or low-cost progress.

### 1.3 FuryLord

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Daily) |
| **Schedule** | 4 spawn windows per day (e.g. 00:00–03:00, 06:00–09:00, 12:00–15:00, 18:00–21:00); does not appear Sunday. |
| **Prerequisites** | Troops, heroes; teleport if troops not auto-summoned. |
| **Requirements** | 4 attacks per day; only **highest single hit** counts for daily leaderboard. |

**Strategies**

- Use **all 4 attacks** (reward track returns fuel after all 4).
- Check **daily bonus faction** (50% damage)—use heroes that match for best hit.
- Use **War Frenzy** on your strongest single hit.
- During **SVS prep**, total damage from **top 200** FuryLord players contributes to state points (up to 30k); coordinate for state ranking.
- Personal achievement (e.g. 3M single hit) can award purple (A-tier) gear.

## 2. Weekly Cycle Events (Repeating Weekly / Multi-day)

### 2.1 Alliance Duel

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Multi-day); 6‑day cycle. |
| **Schedule** | Day 1: Modification. Day 2: Construction. Day 3: Research. Day 4: Heroes. Day 5: Training. Day 6: Combat. Then repeat. |
| **Prerequisites** | Alliance membership. |
| **Requirements** | Contribute points by using the correct items/actions on the correct day (wrenches/blueprints Day 1; construction speed-ups/refugees Day 2; etc.). Unlock up to 9 alliance chests. |

**Strategies**

- **Save then spend on the matching day only:** wrenches, speed-ups, hero fragments, recruitment tickets.
- **Alliance Duel 1st place** = 6,000 SVS prep points daily; **Alliance Duel Victory** = 30,000. Prioritize coordinated saving and spending.
- Sync with **Full Preparedness** themes when they align (e.g., Research day + Age of Science) for double value.
- S4 publishes what to save; S3 coordinates push for 1st place and victory.

---

### 2.2 Zombie Siege

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Weekly) |
| **Schedule** | **Monday or Tuesday** (R4/R5 schedules); duration ~1h 45m or until all bases eliminated. 30 waves; 2 failed defenses = base out. |
| **Prerequisites** | Alliance; all members’ bases can be attacked. |
| **Requirements** | Defend your base; after 2 failures, reinforce others. Personal rewards after all finish; alliance rewards Wednesday after reset. |

**Strategies**

- **Before:** Recall all troops; set strongest wall formation; no shield, mining, digging, boomers/zombies (don’t drain troops).
- **During:** Use **War Frenzy every 10 minutes**. Reinforce **after** failing twice; avoid reinforcing in waves 10–15 so you don’t steal points from lower-power members.
- **Schedule Monday** when possible—wrench rewards align with Alliance Duel Day 1 (Modification).

---

### 2.3 Tyrant

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Weekly) |
| **Schedule** | **2–3 times per week**; ~15 min; R4/R5 set time in Event Center (Apocalypse Time). |
| **Prerequisites** | Alliance; troops at HQ. |
| **Requirements** | Join or lead rallies vs the Tyrant. Primary source of **Steel** (HQ 31–35). |

**Strategies**

- **Recall troops 5 minutes before** start.
- **Only 7–8 member rallies**—never start or join smaller; fill existing rallies before creating new ones.
- Join **nearby** members’ rallies for shorter march and more rallies completed.
- Enable **Engage Offline** if you can’t be online so troops auto-join rallies.
- Use attack buffs and strongest heroes; coordinate in alliance chat so rallies fill fast.

---

### 2.4 Canyon Clash

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Weekly) |
| **Schedule** | Vote Mon–Tue; registration Wed; matchmaking Thu; **combat Fri**; ~1h total (10 min prep + 40 min combat). |
| **Prerequisites** | **Top 20 alliance** by power; **40+ members**; players in alliance **24+ hours**; **15+** registered; Leader/R4 registers. |
| **Requirements** | Capture buildings, defeat Canyon Captain boss, steal energy cells; score points. Matchmaking by top 30 members’ combined power. |

**Strategies**

- Assign **roles:** defenders, cell runners, boss hitters. Use voice comms.
- **Capture buildings early** (military bases ~2,700 first capture, ~1,800/min; medical ~1,800 then ~1,200/min; water ~900 then ~600/min).
- **Energy Station** ~20 min—energy core worth ~100k points; strongest player carries, squads escort.
- **Canyon Captain** spawns ~5th, 15th, 25th min—focus for buffs; teleport when it spawns.
- Free teleports with 3‑min cooldown; use wisely. Injured troops auto-heal after battle.

---

### 2.5 Arena

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Weekly) |
| **Schedule** | Weekly ranking reset. |
| **Prerequisites** | Arena unlocked; attempt tokens. |
| **Requirements** | Win matches to climb ranking. **Arena Champion** gives **20,000 SVS prep points** for the state. |

**Strategies**

- **Final 3 minutes:** many top players push then to avoid revenge and sneak into higher brackets.
- Build **attempt reserves** (diamonds) if close to top 50; rewards (fragments, Valor Medals, skill books) often justify it.
- **Target safer wins**—avoid strong counters or much higher power.
- Coordinate with state for **Arena Champion** during SVS prep to maximize state points.

## 3. Biweekly / SVS Events (Repeating Biweekly)

### 3.1 State Ruler Prep Phase

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Biweekly) |
| **Schedule** | **Sunday–Friday** before each Capital Clash. States earn points; higher score = **Invader**, lower = **Defender**. |
| **Prerequisites** | SVS matchmaking (4 servers grouped). |
| **Requirements** | Earn state points via Alliance Duel (1st 6k/day, Victory 30k), Arena Champion (20k), FuryLord top 200 (up to 30k), raid enemy trucks (100 each), Full Preparedness (500+). |

**Strategies**

- **Max Alliance Duel** 1st place and Victory; **Arena Champion**; coordinate **FuryLord** top 200 and **truck plunder**.
- S2 tracks state score vs opponents; S3/S1 align daily activity so the state wins prep and becomes Invader when possible.
- Don’t overspend resources needed for Full Preparedness and Saturday Capital Clash.

---

### 3.2 Cross-State Capital Clash

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Biweekly) |
| **Schedule** | **Saturday:** Invaders can teleport at 00:00; Capital and turrets exit protection **12:00**; contest **12:00–16:00**; event ends **24:00**. 2–3 weeks, 4–8 states, elimination. |
| **Prerequisites** | Prep phase complete; Invader vs Defender determined. |
| **Requirements** | **Invader:** capture enemy capital (100% progress) or hold highest % at time. **Defender:** prevent 100% capture; hold highest % at time. Turrets: 10 troops each; Capital: 10 troops. Attacking HQs in capital zone = 2× durability loss. |

**Strategies**

- **Invader:** Capture **turrets first** (3 capturable), then push **capital**; mass rallies; use S2 target list; sustain rally tempo.
- **Defender:** Hold turrets and capital; assign defenders (10/turret, 10/capital); shield; state-wide coordination; use 2× durability to attrit invaders.
- **Personal points:** 2.25M total unlocks all 9 boxes (~45k Valor Medals); rally attacks, destroy HQs, reinforce in capital/turret zones. Hospital doubles Saturday—attack to minimize permanent losses.
- **Rewards:** Invader victory = diamond mines, 2 new official alliance positions; personal Valor Medals from boxes.

### 3.3 Enemy Buster / Saturday Buster

| Field | Detail |
|-------|--------|
| **Type** | Repeating (Biweekly) |
| **Schedule** | Saturday during Capital Clash; ends 24:00. |
| **Prerequisites** | SVS active. |
| **Requirements** | Earn personal points by killing enemies in capital/turret zones (rally attacks, destroy HQs, reinforce, TvT). |

**Strategies**

- **2.25M points** = all 9 personal boxes (~45k Valor Medals); possible even if state loses.
- Rotation: start with reinforce/TvT; mid-event snipe 5–10 low HQs; end with capital rallies for burst.
- Position HQ in brown dirt (level 24+ ideal); stock heals, teleports, RSS. Sync with Alliance VS for 18 total boxes (double Valor Medals).
